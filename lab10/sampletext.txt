Its not just that each of us is more delicately choosing our characters knowing that an exclamation point or a colon carries more weight in our character world Or even that our punctuation suddenly feels like hyperbole because weve lost all audible tone

Those things are true But its also as if a kind of micropunctuation has emerged tiny marks in the smallest of spaces that suddenly tell us more about the person on the other end than the words themselves or at least we think they do
